Adlucem Architecture Website Case Study

This project aimed to design a modern, user-friendly website for Adlucem, an architecture and design firm. The goal was to showcase their portfolio, highlight featured projects, and provide a seamless user experience for potential clients.

Design Goals

  • Highlight Adlucem's innovative projects and design philosophy.
  • Provide easy navigation for visitors to explore the latest projects.
  • Create a professional yet engaging interface that reflects the brand's identity.

Process

Starting with user research and a thorough understanding of the brand, I created wireframes to plan the layout and structure. These wireframes helped ensure that the navigation was intuitive, and the visuals stood out without overwhelming the user.

Final Design

The final design implemented a clean, modern interface with a focus on large imagery to showcase projects. Typography was chosen for readability and to maintain a professional aesthetic. Special attention was given to responsive design to ensure accessibility across all devices.
